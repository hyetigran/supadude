# Score is Coins collected, not deaths

Issue #6 originally defined Score as the number of deaths (Checkpoint respawns) accumulated while completing the Level, lower being better, with Coins explicitly carved out as a separate "collected/total" completion stat that did not affect Score or Leaderboard ranking. In practice this meant Score never moved during a typical Attempt unless the player died — nothing rewarded skillful play or collection, and the number labeled "Score" and shown live during a clean run just sat at 0, reading as broken rather than as feedback. We redefined Score as the running count of Coins collected (higher is better), tie-broken by completion time same as before. Lives/Checkpoint-respawn behavior is unchanged — dying still costs a Life and still triggers a Checkpoint respawn — it just no longer feeds Score. This also collapses Score and the Coins "collected/total" completion stat into the same underlying number, and flips the Leaderboard's ranking direction from fewest-deaths to most-coins.
